# Statistics Reading Guide

## Primary Books
- **Mathematical Statistics and Data Analysis (Rice)**: Core theory for estimation bounds and formal testing.
- **Mathematics for Machine Learning (MML)**: Chapter 8 (When Models Meet Data) - bridges stats to ML modeling.

---

## Subtopic References

### `01-estimation`
- **Rice**: Chapter 8 (Estimation - Maximum Likelihood, Confidence Intervals, Properties of Estimators).
- **MML**: Section 8.3 (Parameter Estimation). Understand MLE (Maximum Likelihood Estimation) and MAP (Maximum A Posteriori).

### `02-hypothesis-testing`
- **Rice**: Chapter 9 (Testing Hypotheses).

### `03-bias-variance-tradeoff`
- **Rice**: Read concepts heavily related to estimator bias and variance in Chapter 8.
- **MML**: Section 8.2 (Empirical Risk Minimization) and Section 8.4 (Evaluating Models) - relates estimation errors to underfitting/overfitting.
