# Probability Reading Guide

## Primary Books
- **Introduction to Probability (Blitzstein)**: Detailed intuition, classical probability, and statistical foundations.
- **Mathematics for Machine Learning (MML)**: Chapter 6 (Probability and Distributions) - for quick ML-context mapping.

---

## Subtopic References

### `01-bayes-theorem-and-events`
- **Blitzstein**: Chapter 1 (Probability & Counting) & Chapter 2 (Conditional Probability, Bayes' Theorem).
- **MML**: Sections 6.1 - 6.3 (Probability Spaces, Sum & Product Rules, Bayes).

### `02-random-variables-and-expectation`
- **Blitzstein**: Chapter 3 (Random Variables) & Chapter 4 (Expectation).
- **MML**: Section 6.4 (Expectation, Variance, and Covariance).

### `03-key-distributions`
- **Blitzstein**: Covered heavily in Chapter 5 (Continuous Random Variables like Gaussian) and Chapter 6.
- **MML**: Section 6.5 (Common Probability Distributions - Bernoulli, Gaussian) & Section 6.6 (Conjugate Priors).
