# Linear Algebra Reading Guide

This folder matches the recommended multi-book sequence for your AI math foundation.

## Core Sequence
1. **Mathematics for Machine Learning (MML)**: Broad foundation and ML focus.
2. **Linear Algebra Done Right (LADR)**: Selective reading for deep theoretical rigor on vector spaces and eigen theory.
3. **Linear Algebra and Learning from Data (Strang)**: Practical matrix factorization, SVD, and data-driven perspective.

---

## Subtopic References

### `01-vectors-and-matrices`
- **MML**: Chapter 2 (Sections 2.1 - 2.3) focus on basic operations.
- **Strang**: Part I.1 (Matrix-Vector Multiplication).

### `02-vector-spaces`
- **MML**: Chapter 2 (Sections 2.4 - 2.7) linear independence, basis, norms.
- **LADR**: Chapter 1 (Vector Spaces) and Chapter 2 (Finite-Dimensional Vector Spaces). *Use this for theoretical depth.*

### `03-matrix-multiplication-transformations`
- **MML**: Chapter 2 (Section 2.7 - Linear Mappings).
- **LADR**: Chapter 3 (Linear Maps).
- **Strang**: Part I.2 (Linear Algebra) focuses heavily on the 4 fundamental subspaces.

### `04-eigenvalues-and-eigenvectors`
- **MML**: Chapter 4 (Matrix Decompositions, 4.1 Eigendecomposition, 4.2 SVD).
- **LADR**: Chapter 5 (Eigenvalues, Eigenvectors, and Invariant Subspaces).
- **Strang**: Part I.3 (Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors) and Part III (Low Rank and SVD).
